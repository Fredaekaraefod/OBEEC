Quality Food

At OBEEC, we take pride in serving quality Nigerian food made with the freshest and finest ingredients. Our menu features a wide range of authentic Nigerian dishes that are expertly prepared by our skilled chefs to ensure every meal is flavorful and satisfying. Whether you're in the mood for jollof rice, egusi soup, or suya, we have something for everyone. Come and indulge in our quality Nigerian food that's sure to leave you wanting more.

Online Order

At OBEEC, we make it easy and convenient for you to place your order online. Our user-friendly online ordering system allows you to customize your order and select your preferred pickup or delivery time. You can place your order from the comfort of your home or on-the-go using your mobile device. With just a few clicks, your delicious Nigerian meal will be on its way to you.

24/7 Service

At OBEEC, we understand that hunger can strike at any time, day or night, and that's why we offer 24/7 service. Our doors are always open, and our dedicated team is always ready to serve you, whenever you want it. Whether you're craving a late-night bowl of pepper soup or an early morning breakfast of akara and pap, we've got you covered. With our 24/7 service, you can enjoy delicious Nigerian meals at any time of the day or night, any day of the week.
